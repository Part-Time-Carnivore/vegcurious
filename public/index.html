Vegcurious

This is a simple app that you can use to track the variety of vegetables that you eat.

Your daily veg will be saved in this browser, but is not currently saved to a database, so you will not be able to access your data elsewhere.

I'm at the very early stages of developing this app, so please try it out and feel free to suggest changes.
